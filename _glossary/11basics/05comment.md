## Tag

`<!--WDK:comment:value-->`

## Description

Use this tag to make a comment on your code. 
Comments will not appear on the published site, but may be useful for your reference and organisation.

## Example

~~~
<!--WDK:comment:The following section is the site's footer.-->
~~~
